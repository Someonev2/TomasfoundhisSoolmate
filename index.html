General Q & A

About Our Venue:

Q: "Will I need to travel between the ceremony and reception?"

A: Good news! Everything's happening at the same gorgeous venue (unless there's any last-minute surprises! 😉)

-

Q: "Inside or outside ceremony?"

A: We'll be celebrating indoors! Just a heads up - seating during the ceremony will be a bit cozy. We're saving seats for those who need them most (like our immediate families and guests who might need extra comfort). Don't worry though - we're working on squeezing in as many chairs as possible! The reception will have plenty of seats for everyone! 💺

-

Q: "Help! Where should I park?"

A: Here's a little insider tip - Saturday parking is FREE! 🚗 Your best bet is around Honeysuckle (just a lovely 9-minute stroll away). If you're feeling lucky, you might snag a spot on Hunter Street!

-

All About Our Guests:

Q: "Can I bring the little ones?"

A: We'd love to see your kiddos at the ceremony! But for the reception, we're making it an adults-only party night. 🎉

-

Style Guide:

Q: "What should I wear?"

A: Think cocktail party meets smart casual! Just one tiny request - we'd love if you could skip the wine/maroon coloured outfits as our wonderful bridal party will be rocking those shades! 👗

-

Q: "Is there a specific theme I should know about?"

A: We're keeping it simple and letting our beautiful venue (with all its warm, cozy colours) do the talking! ✨

-

Food, Drinks & Presents:

Q: "What's on the menu?"

A: We're going with delicious canapés to keep the party flowing! And who knows? If we're all still peckish, we might throw in some extra treats as the night goes on! 🍽️

-

Q: "What about drinks?"

A: Good news - there's an open bar tab! 🥂 Once that runs out, you're welcome to keep the party going by purchasing your own drinks. We want everyone to have a fantastic time while staying safe!

-

Q: "About wedding gifts..."

A: Rather than a registry, we've set up a sweet little Wishing Well for our honeymoon adventures! But honestly, your presence is the best present! 🎁

-

Got more questions? We'd love to hear from you! Don't be shy - just reach out to us directly! We're here to help make this celebration amazing for everyone! 💕
